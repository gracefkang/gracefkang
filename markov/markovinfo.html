this is a javascript/html project using markov chain generation !
i used an archive of various text sources as the input to mimic multiple writing styles

the main constraint for the project was that the accuracy of the generator was dependent upon the length of the sample .txt file, and i couldn't use extremely large files due to loading times and such. thus, the generators are a bit nonsensical, but funny nonetheless. i used shakespeare, trump, kanye, and dril as sources because they are all twitter accounts that have a distinct writing style and tend not to make sense on their own, so the limitations of the markov generator wouldn't be as noticable/much of a problem.
